Hiking & Camping

When the oppotunity arrises I really enjoy getting outside for a hike with friends. Most of the national parks I have visited I saw during my recent roadtrip and I am looking forward to seeing more soon. My favorite hike thus far was Angel's Landing in Zion National Park.

Skiing & Snowboarding

Once the temperature drops and the snow starts to fall I hit the slopes for skiing and snowboarding. I like doing both depending on the conditions and am looking to get more time in this winter. I actually broke my nose skiing when I was young and lost most of my sense of smell.

Soccer

Being from the US I enjoy playing soccer although I haven't played for a club team in several years. I played right defense and try to get out to play pickup as much as possible with friends.
